I'm a full stack software developer
from Nashville, TN

Born in Nashville, I've worked as a recording engineer and music producer, a music supervisor, and an electronics technician. As each job took me in a slightly more technical direciton, I began to realize that my tinkering habits could be honed and put to better use. I've worked with software in every job I've had, why not work ON software as well? After studying web development on my own for a year, I decided to dive in and jumpstart my career in software by enrolling in a 6 month full stack bootcamp at Nashville Software School. I've loved what I've learned, and I'm always curious to dig deeper into the whys and hows of technologies I know while exploring new and foreign languages and expanding my ability.

Projects

TipHat

How much are you making for your favorite artists by streaming their music? TipHat is a React + Material-UI app that uses your last.fm account to show the user a personalized breakdown of their listening history, and how that has affected the artist. Music has never been cheaper, and artists are struggling to make ends meet. This app aims to spread awareness with data.
